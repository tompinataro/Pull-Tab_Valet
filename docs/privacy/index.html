Pull Tab Valet — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 17, 2026

Pull Tab Valet respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Email address associated with your organization account.
  • Visit Submissions: Task checklist items, notes, and completion details you provide during service visits.
  • Approximate Location (Optional): If you grant permission, we may collect approximate location at check-in or check-out to verify presence. The app works without location access unless your organization requires it.
  • Diagnostic Information: Basic server logs, sync errors, and operational data used to maintain reliability. We do not use third-party advertising SDKs.

How We Use Information

  • Provide and operate the service (authenticate users, display routes, record visit submissions, and sync field activity).
  • Improve reliability and support (debug issues, verify submissions, and resolve account or sync problems).

Sharing

We do not sell your data. We may share data with service providers who help us operate the service, such as hosting or database providers, under appropriate safeguards.

Security

All network communication uses HTTPS/TLS. Authentication tokens are stored securely on device and transmitted only over encrypted connections.

Retention

Visit submissions are retained according to your organization’s policy. Diagnostic logs are retained only as long as needed to operate and support the service.

Children’s Privacy

Pull Tab Valet is intended for use by professional field technicians and operators and is not directed to children under 13.

Your Choices

  • Location Permission: You can deny or revoke location access in your device settings; the app remains functional unless your organization specifically requires it.
  • Account & Data Requests: Use https://tompinataro.github.io/Pull-Tab-Valet/support#account-deletion for account updates or data deletion requests.

Contact

Email TixPy@pinataro.com for questions, support, or privacy requests.
