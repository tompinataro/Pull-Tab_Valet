Pull Tab Valet — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 18, 2026

Pull Tab Valet respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Email address and related account details used to sign in and manage access.
  • Operational Records: Venue details, client location details, pull-tab box records, scanned UPCs, deposits, prizes, notes, and related closeout or report data you enter in the app.
  • Camera Access: If you grant permission, the app can use your camera to scan pull-tab box UPCs.
  • Location Information (Optional): If you grant permission, the app may use device location to help place or update client locations and support location-aware workflows.
  • Diagnostic Information: Basic logs, sync errors, and operational data used to maintain reliability and troubleshoot issues.

How We Use Information

  • Provide and operate the service, including authentication, venue and box management, scanning, closeouts, and reporting.
  • Sync records across devices and backend systems.
  • Maintain reliability, troubleshoot issues, and respond to support requests.

Sharing

We do not sell your data. We may share data with service providers that help us host, store, transmit, or support the service, subject to appropriate safeguards.

Security

Network communication uses HTTPS/TLS. Authentication tokens and related credentials are stored and transmitted using standard security controls appropriate to the platform.

Retention

Operational records are retained as needed to provide the service, maintain reporting history, and satisfy business or legal obligations. Diagnostic logs are retained only as long as reasonably needed for support and reliability.

Children’s Privacy

Pull Tab Valet is intended for adult business or organizational use and is not directed to children under 13.

Your Choices

  • Camera Permission: You can deny or revoke camera access in device settings, but UPC scanning will not work without it.
  • Location Permission: You can deny or revoke location access in device settings. Core app features remain available except for workflows that rely on location input.
  • Account & Data Requests: Use https://tompinataro.github.io/Pull-Tab_Valet/support#account-deletion for account updates or data deletion requests.

Contact

Email TixPy@pinataro.com for questions, support, or privacy requests.
